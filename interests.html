Baking

My Favorite Carrot Cake

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup apple sauce
  • ¾ cup olive oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups shredded carrots

    • Dry Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp cloves
  • ¼ tsp ginger

    • Frosting Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup soften butter
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • Splash of vanilla extract

    • Cake Steps

    1. In a large bowl mix all of the wet ingredients minus the carrots
    2. Next add the dry ingredients into the bowl and whisk togethe
    3. Fold in carrots into the mixture
    4. Separate the mixture evenly into two 9” round baking tins. Place into oven at 350º for 35 minutes

    Frosting Steps

    1. Combine frosting ingredients into mixer and mix until forms stiff peaks
    2. Spread a ½ in layer of frosting in between the two layers of cooled cake
    3. Frost the outside of the cake until evenly coated. Add any extra decor to cake
    4. Serve and Enjoy!!
